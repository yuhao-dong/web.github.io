City College of New York Microfluidic Research

In this research, we were focused on designing, making, and improving multilayered flow focusing microcapillary devices for both single and double monodisperse emulsion droplets. We analyzed the currently existing devices that can make the monodisperse emulsion droplets, and then re-designed a new device which could improve the shortage of the current one, and make the device easy to make and assemble, and could potentially being massively manufactured. We also analyzed the properties of our new device, gathered all parameters, and created a data sheet that lists the relationship between the droplet size, type of injection, and injection press, and all other properties.
